Assumptions Behind These Targets

(1) UK users behave similarly to US at launch - this may not hold and will be calibrated against real data within the first 30 days.  (2) CPI targets assume a mobile-first casual gaming audience, not a high-intent searcher cohort - the blend will shift as verticals are added.  (3) D7 ROAS targets assume the game and survey offer inventory is competitive at UK launch - if inventory quality is below US standard, ROAS will be lower. All targets will be reassessed at Day 30 against real cohort data.
