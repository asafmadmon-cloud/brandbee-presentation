Two Systems Needed to Build Play Store Credibility from Zero

System 1 - In-App Rating Prompt

Trigger Google Play rating prompt after first reward event - the moment of highest user satisfaction. Timing is critical: prompt shown too early (before earning) backfires. Target: reach 4.0+ rating within 90 days.

System 2 - Google Play Support Infrastructure

Dedicated team to respond to UK Play Store reviews within 24h. R&D bug fix SLA agreed before launch. Before building features, conduct structured research on UK earning preferences and reach out to former employees at established UK cashback platforms to understand historical friction points.
