Fraud: Four Lines of Defence

Reward app fraud doesn't just waste budget - it destroys partner relationships. Four layers deployed from Day 1.

Layer 1

Block Before It Enters (Network Level)

  • AppsFlyer Protect360 blocks known botnets and click-injection farms
  • Blocked before the install is recorded
  • First and most cost-effective defence line

Layer 2

Catch at the Door (Geo and Identity)

  • Strict enforcement: IP geolocation ≠ GAID-registered region → flagged
  • Users masking location to access higher-paying UK offers blocked
  • Flagged installs → manual review queue

Layer 3

Monitor Post-Install (Behavioural)

  • Time-between-levels: Level 10 in 4 min vs. 4-hr cohort average = emulator
  • Zero-click patterns inside BrandBee UI
  • Survey attention check failures · Account ban within 24h

Layer 4 - External Fraud Specialist

External fraud specialist · First 30 days

Fraudulent completions trigger aggregator clawbacks and reduce revenue-share rates. One fraud wave can set back partner trust by months. The cost of prevention is a fraction of remediation - and it protects the entire dataset on which our D90 gate decision is based.
