Defining the High-Quality User

HQU Threshold

Score >75 / 100 · UK Target: >30% of cohort by D90

Dimension 1

40%

Data Integrity

  • Receipt scan accuracy >85% - legible, valid, non-duplicate
  • Survey consistency >80% - no straight-lining or speeding

Weighted highest: inaccurate data triggers partner audits - existential risk to the business model

Dimension 2

35%

Engagement Depth

  • >4 sessions/week - habit formation indicator
  • Cross-vertical: 1 receipt scan + 1 game download in 14 days

Cross-vertical users: 2.4× higher LTV

Dimension 3

25%

Revenue Contribution

  • D30 ROAS >50% - on track to break-even by D60
  • First withdrawal completed - the "Aha!" moment

Weighted lowest: revenue from fraud triggers clawbacks - data integrity must come first

HQU Formula: (Data Integrity × 0.40) + (Engagement Depth × 0.35) + (Revenue Contribution × 0.25) > 75 → classified as High-Quality User

Data Integrity is weighted highest because BrandBee's user data feeds Similarweb's broader intelligence ecosystem. Low-quality users don't just hurt app economics - they corrupt the data asset.
