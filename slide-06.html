The 90-Day Approach

Each phase has a clear entry condition and a clear exit gate. No phase begins until the previous one is confirmed.

Phase 0

Foundation

Days 1–14

  • Fix all product gaps
  • Confirm tracking infrastructure
  • Secure CSM contacts at all channels
  • Align KPIs with leadership

NO UA SPEND

Phase 1

First Signal

Days 15–30

  • Google UAC/Gaming only
  • Low scale - validate funnel end-to-end
  • Establish baseline CPI
  • Track D1 retention & D7 ROAS

Phase 2

Scale

Days 31–60

  • Expand to AppLovin, Meta, TikTok
  • Open Surveys and Receipts verticals
  • Shift to tROAS bidding
  • Scale winning channels

Phase 3

Optimise

Days 61–90

  • Scale proven channels
  • Launch influencer programme
  • Prepare Gate 4 decision
  • Net LTV/CAC >1.5× threshold

Rule: Google UAC only in Phase 1 - no additional channel complexity until tracking is end-to-end validated

Rule: TikTok Spark Ads and micro-influencer programme only from Phase 2–3 - not Phase 1
