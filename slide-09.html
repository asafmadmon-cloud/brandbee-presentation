The 4 Decision Gates

The decision to scale or pivot is not a judgement call on Day 90. It is a mathematical outcome of four pre-agreed thresholds.

1

Gate 1 · Day 7

Acquisition Efficiency

Metric: Blended CPI across all channels

↑ CPI <$0.80 + D7 ROAS >25% → +30% budget

↓ CPI >$1.20 + ROAS <15% → pause UA

2

Gate 2 · Day 30

Core Retention

Metric: D30 Retention vs. US Baseline

↑ UK D30 ≥ US baseline → unlock Meta + AppLovin

↓ >30% below US → pause UA, fix product

3

Gate 3 · Day 45

Partner Value

Metric: Game Download Rate from aggregator clicks

↑ >50% download + IAP matches US → direct deals

↓ <15% download → trust gap diagnosed

4

Gate 4 · Day 90

The Call

Metric: Net LTV/CAC — Partner Revenue — Rewards / UA Spend

↑ >1.5× → DOUBLE DOWN — unlock Phase 2 budget

↓ <1.0× → PIVOT — teardown report within 2 weeks

Important: Gate 4 uses NET ROI = (Partner Revenue - Rewards Earned) / UA Spend. Gross ROI can look healthy while rewards liability accumulates. Net is the true economic health indicator.
