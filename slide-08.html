Section B - 4 UK Creative Directions

"The Triple Earner" - TikTok/Meta Reels · 15 sec UGC

Person on Tube platform scans receipt, completes a survey, downloads a game - all before their train arrives. Hook: "I made £X on my commute." Why it works: Shows the multi-vertical loop in one session. British commuter context = instant relatability. Addresses "is it worth my time?" doubt before it forms.

"Receipt to Reward" - Meta Carousel · Shopper audience

Frame 1: Tesco/Sainsbury's receipt. Frame 2: Phone scan. Frame 3: Points added. Frame 4: Cash to PayPal. No narration - visual proof only. Why it works: UK supermarket recognition removes trust barrier immediately. Specific retailer imagery drives click-through from existing shoppers.

"Level Up, Cash Out" - Playable Ad · AppLovin/Unity

User plays 30 seconds of a game inside the ad, completes a level, and BrandBee points appear in real time. CTA: "That level just earned you 50p." Why it works: Demonstrates the value loop inside the ad itself. No imagination gap. Competitor gap - no UK reward app runs playable ads.

"Transparent Payout" - Screen Recording · Trust-builder

No voiceover, no script. Real user: open app, complete task, hit withdraw, show bank transfer arriving. Why it works: UK scepticism requires proof, not promise. Screen recording is the highest-trust format. Directly counters the #1 barrier: "is this a scam?"
